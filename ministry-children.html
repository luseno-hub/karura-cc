Kisima Champions

Kisima Champions ministry epitomizes the journey towards becoming ‘Christ like and making Jesus known’. We seek to present Christ in age appropriate curriculum, application oriented approach, and in the context of intentional shepherding and mentoring. Children are encouraged to commit their lives to Jesus, live for Him and make Him known.

This ministry has tailored its program to suit children between 2 to 12 years and address various differentials. This gives the children a rare opportunity to experience worship and to grow among believers from diverse backgrounds. We are strategically positioned to nurture the next blessed generation to the glory of God. Teams of volunteers’ minister rotationally to over 800 children every Sunday.

Primary School Visits School visits take place weekly during school term.
Vacation Bible School (VBS) This is an annual weeklong event that takes place every school holiday.
Bible Olympics This is an annual weeklong event that tests our Children’s’ knowledge of the Bible through fun interactive games with prizes and vital lessons.
December Christmas Musical Children start practicing for the musical in September in preparation for presentation on the first Sunday of every December.
Childrens Prayer Day This is an annual event.
