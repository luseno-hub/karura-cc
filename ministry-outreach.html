Outreach

scroll

Becoming Christ-like Disciples

The mission of the Missions & Outreach ministry is to know Christ and to make Him known derived from our Church Vision: Becoming Christ-like disciples engaging the world. The ministry endeavors congregational participation through encouraging attendees to live out their Christian witness and specifically target non-believing friends and acquaintances. Institutional Outreach is also a key aspect of the ministry. The institutions are prisons, schools and colleges.

Prisons & rehabs

As a community that believes in obedience to the great commission, we conduct Sunday and weekly services, leadership training, discipleship programs, reconciliation, and counseling to our brothers and sisters in Prisons. We also help more than 14,000 inmates in over 20 prisons across the country integrate back to society upon release.
